Design Principles Document

WDD230 - Esther F. Segifili

Repetition

Remax

The design principle of Repetition is clearly shown on this webpage through its use of colors. The colors red and blue are repeated throughout the page as well as the fonts.

Contrast

National Geographic

The National Geographic page uses the design principle of Contrast as illustrated in their use of the color gold against the black. Using a bright color such as gold to contrast against the black makes certain elements of the page pop out to the viewer.

Proximity

Pocono Whitewater

This webpage uses headings and spaces to emphasize the relationship between the texts on the page. This use of Proximity shows which headings and sections belong together and then space to show that a new section is about to begin.
