Site Name

Local Treasures Commerce

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase the variety of local businesses that are in American Samoa. This site will be a place where local businesses can promote their business, invite and encourage the residents as well as visitors to buy local. This website will also include community and business highlights, local calendar events, local weather, directory, historical background of American Samoa, and current demographics.
